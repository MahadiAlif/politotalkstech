The Data Science & Analytics Hub connects students interested in extracting insights from data. We work with real datasets, learn statistical techniques, and build data-driven solutions to real problems.

## Skill Development

Our curriculum covers:

- Statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
- Data visualization and storytelling
- Machine learning for data analysis
- Big data technologies and tools
- Business intelligence and analytics

## Real-World Projects

We collaborate with:

- Local businesses on analytics projects
- Research labs on data analysis
- Non-profits on impact measurement
- Student organizations on data-driven decision making

## Tools and Technologies

Members gain experience with:

- Python data science stack
- R for statistical computing
- SQL and database management
- Tableau and visualization tools
- Big data frameworks

## Competition Teams

We participate in:

- Data science competitions
- Analytics case competitions
- Visualization challenges
- Research paper reproductions

## Industry Speakers

Regular talks from data professionals covering topics like data engineering, analytics in industry, and career paths in data science.
